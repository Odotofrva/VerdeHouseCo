About Verdé House

A luxury cannabis house built around taste, atmosphere, and intentional discovery.

Verdé House is envisioned as a space where premium cannabis is paired with design-forward presentation, warm service, and a slower, more thoughtful sense of ritual. Every detail is meant to feel elevated without losing its sense of comfort.

Curated flower, elevated hospitality, and a house identity made to linger.
